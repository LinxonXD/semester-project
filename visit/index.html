Visit

Location and parking

The museum is located at Osloveien 1, 4879 Grimstad. We offer free parking for all visitors in the parking lot at the back of the building or in the parking garage across the street.

Opening hours

Monday: Closed

Tuesday: 10:00 - 16:00

Wednesday: 10:00 - 16:00

Thursday: 10:00 - 16:00

Friday: 10:00 - 19:00

Saturday: 09:00 - 16:00

Sunday: 09:00 - 13:00

Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a printed guide of the museum.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drinks

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.
