Get involved

Help shape the future of science

The Community Science Museum is always looking for passionate people and partners who want to be part of what we do.

Whether you are interested in supporting our work, volunteering your time, or gaining experience through and internship, there are many ways to contribute.

Together, we can inspire curiosity and make science accessible to everyone in our community.

Support

There are various ways you can support the museum. Donations are very welcome and are an important way we keep this museum open and accessible to the whole community.

You can also support us by donating items of interest to the museum's collections. If you have some item or collection that you think others would enjoy, please let us know by contaction our Collections Department and they will be able to assist you.

Volunteer

A number of people volunteer their time and effort to keep the displays in good order and ready for visitors to come and enjoy. Volunteering has its perks including getting to see behind the scenes of a working museum, access to staff-only lectures, and a monthly lunch where all staff and volunteers come together to discuss ideas for future exhibits and strategies for the museum.

You can help colunteer in a number of different spheres. Please contact us if you'd like to find out more about how you can get involved.

Internships

Are you interested in working in a museum? Do you enjoy the fun and excitement of sharing the wonders of nature with people? Well you could be just the right person to enjoy an internship at the museum.

You'll be learning from a numver of different acedemics and people who are passionate about science and sharing it with the wider community.
